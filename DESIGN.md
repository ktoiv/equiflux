# Equiflux — Financial Intelligence Dashboard

## Vibe & Philosophy
Equiflux is a high-fidelity stock analysis platform that balances sophisticated data density with the calm, minimalist aesthetics of the Hermes Finance design system. It follows a modular, three-panel architecture designed for professional market analysis without visual overwhelm.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** #F8F6F0 (warm parchment)
- **Surface:** #FFFFFF (pure white cards and surfaces)
- **Primary (Sage):** #A8D5BA (used for headers, primary actions, and positive trends)
- **Secondary (Blue):** #B8D4E3 (used for volume data and secondary accents)
- **Accent (Blush):** #F0C4C4 (used for alerts and negative indicators)
- **Text Primary:** #2D3436 (high-contrast near-black)
- **Text Secondary:** #636E72 (muted gray for labels)
- **Border/Outline:** #E2E8E4 (subtle dividers)

## Typography
- **Font:** Plus Jakarta Sans (Rounded, humanist sans-serif)
- **Display (Price):** 32px-40px, Semibold
- **H2 (Stock Title):** 24px, Semibold
- **Body-MD (Data Labels):** 16px, Regular
- **Label-MD (Headers/Stats):** 14px, Medium/Semibold, often Uppercase
- **Label-SM (Annotations):** 12px, Regular

## Spacing & Layout
- **Grid:** 4px baseline grid
- **Desktop Layout:** Three-panel fixed-fluid architecture (Sidebar: 280px | Center: Fluid | Analysis Panel: 360px)
- **Gutter:** 24px between panels
- **Outer Margins:** 32px from viewport edge
- **Internal Padding:** 24px minimum for cards

## Components
- **Sidebar Selector:** Borderless list with Level 0 subtle backgrounds; primary-container (#A8D5BA) for active states.
- **Data Card:** Level 1 shadow, 16px corner radius, white background.
- **Time Toggle:** Pill-shaped (9999px), label-md typography.
- **Indicator Stack:** Compact cards featuring large-scale values and uppercase labels.
- **Primary Button:** Sage background (#A8D5BA), dark text, 8px radius, no shadow.
- **Trend Badges:** Pill-shaped with sage (positive) or blush (negative) tints.

## Interaction Language
- **Hover:** Surface-container-high (#E8E8E5)
- **Active:** Surface-container-highest (#E2E3DF)
- **Focus:** White background with 1px Sage border
- **Shadows:** Level 1 (Ambient) for standard cards; Level 2 (Defined) for modals.
